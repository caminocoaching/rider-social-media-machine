## Canva Workflow

### AI Image + Text Overlay (Facebook/Instagram Feed)
1. Open Canva, create 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1350 (4:5 for Instagram)
2. Upload AI image as background
3. Add dark gradient overlay (black, 40-60% opacity) on bottom third
4. Place hook text in white (bold sans-serif, 48-64pt)
5. Export as PNG

### Text-on-Black (Highest Engagement for Data Hooks)
1. Background: Pure black (#000000)
2. White text, centred, maximum 3 lines
3. Font: Montserrat Black, Arial Black, or Impact
4. 1200x1200 for Facebook, 1080x1350 for Instagram
5. Export as PNG
6. Time: 90 seconds

### Instagram Carousel
1. Slide 1: AI image or text-on-black with hook
2. Slides 2-5: Dark background (#0A1628) with white text, one point per slide
3. Slide 6: CTA with lead magnet name and trigger word
4. Font: consistent across all slides
5. Time: 5-8 minutes for full carousel
