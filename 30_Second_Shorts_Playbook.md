## Core Rules Summary

1. **1.5s hook window** — 5-7 words on screen, voice at 0.0s
2. **Text on screen is mandatory** — 60% watch sound-off
3. **One idea per video** — 4 slides, 3 sections
4. **Slide change every 3-5s** — visual micro-changes within slides
5. **Design for the loop** — ending connects to opening
6. **Avatar earns its space** — gestures, expressions, eye contact
7. **Audio is a second hook** — fast/slow/direct pacing rhythm
8. **CTA is effortless** — "Comment MINDSET for the free quiz" (6 words)
9. **Batch production** — 7-10 shorts in 2 hours
10. **Measure and repeat** — 70% 3s retention, 60% avg duration

## The 4-Slide Structure

- **Slide 1 (0-5s)**: HOOK — 5-7 words, scroll-stopping stat/claim
- **Slide 2 (5-15s)**: INSIGHT — Article ref + chemical explanation (35-40 words)
- **Slide 3 (15-25s)**: PROOF — One big data point (15-20 words)
- **Slide 4 (25-30s)**: CTA — "Comment MINDSET for the free quiz"

**Total: 75-85 words spoken**
